Your Supervillain Personality is:

Lord Voldemort

You are a genius mastermind with highly analytical skills, and you are completely motivated by power and control. Your only ambition in life is to become an all-powerful, all-controlling immortal. You will stop at nothing to achieve your goals and will stop anyone who gets in your way. You are a powerful leader, needing loyal followers to thrive and serve your every need!

Cruella de Vil

You are a cunning, witty, and intelligent leader. You thrive under pressure because you believe that the more times you fall, the more times you will rise. You have a strong desire for a life of luxury, and are determined to acquire your goals through power and control. One thing is for certain, you have a great sense of style!

The Joker

You are persistent, fearless, and highly intelligent, but you also love being the centre of attention. You are thorough and always ensure that your plans will turn out the way they were supposed to! In the past, you were frequently misunderstood, and have become a villain as a result of being wronged when you were too kind or naive. You never give up and too many failures may also be hurtful to your mental health in the end. You can also be a little crazy and unpredictable, but that's what makes people drawn to you!

The Green Goblin

You are extremely ambitious and business-minded, and have become self-made despite difficulties in your childhood. You are also highly skilled, particularly with gadgets, which you use to achieve your goals and enhance your performance. You are also a masterful strategist, but have created a masked personality that has superseded your normal self. You can achieve great things in life, if you refocus your energy towards something good!

Thanos

You are an extremely focused person, and are completely set on pursuing your goals, no matter the cost. You are highly skilled at getting others to do what you want, but can be destructive, and tend to lack empathy if you hurt someone along the way. You know how powerful you are, and often act on impulse because it brings you a lot of excitement. On a more positive note, you don't discriminate against others, and are not concerned with power or status, only accomplishing your goals no matter what.

Vecna

Like Vecna from Stranger Things, you are known for your ambition and drive, never letting your confidence fall even when others doubt you. You have a strong belief in your abilities, which makes you seem invincible to the people around you. With your psychic-telekinetic powers, you can move things with your mind and enter people's memories, which makes you incredibly skilled and powerful. We hope you use these talents for good in the future!

Maleficent

Although you can be sinister, you thrive to excel and love being the best at everything you do. In fact, so much so that you will make sure not a single soul will get in the way of you accomplishing your dreams. On the downside, when failure or betrayal comes your way, you tend to react with vengeance, rather than with love. However, even though you don't like to admit it, you also have a caring side that wants to protect the innocent, and you will fiercely protect them at all costs.

Magneto

Despite being a villain, you are a legend even amongst Superheroes! Protective, determined, and aggressively assertive, your goal is save mutant-kind from humanity, no matter the cost. In the past, you followed a code of ethics, but because of your efficiency, you are willing to put ethics aside to get the job done. Despite this, you still believe in a future where we will all get along, and are focused on achieving that at a later stage in the game. Your power, despite being used for sinister reasons, is still respectable!
